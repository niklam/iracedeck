Camera Cycle lets you step through iRacing's various camera groups and car selections without opening any menus.

## Modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Next Camera | Switch to the next camera group |
| Previous Camera | Switch to the previous camera group |
| Next Sub-Camera | Switch to the next sub-camera within the current group |
| Previous Sub-Camera | Switch to the previous sub-camera within the current group |
| Next Car | Switch focus to the next car |
| Previous Car | Switch focus to the previous car |
| Next Driving Camera | Switch to the next driving-style camera |
| Previous Driving Camera | Switch to the previous driving-style camera |

## Encoder Support

Yes — rotation cycles through cameras in the chosen mode, giving quick one-handed camera control with the Stream Deck+ dial.
