## Car Setup Adjustments

> **Note:** Settings ending with "Set" require two button bindings in iRacing — one for increase, one for decrease. In the iRacing UI, you'll see a single row that accepts two bindings (e.g., "Brake Bias Set" shows two binding slots). Settings ending with "Toggle" or "Hold" only require a single button.
